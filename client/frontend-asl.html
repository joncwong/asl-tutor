About ASL Tutor


ASL Tutor is an AI-assistant that intends to help users learn how to perform correct alphabet signs in ASL through games and correctness test.


Although there are many variations of sign language, American Sign Language (ASL) serves as the most popular sign language in the deaf community. Learning it is essential for those who want to be able to communicate with those that are deaf.
